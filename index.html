Ghostline

A subscription-based SaaS platform providing virtual number and call management services.

What Ghostline does

  • Provides a virtual number for receiving calls
  • Call routing / forwarding to your real phone
  • Call logs & basic call history inside the app
  • Monthly subscription plans

How it works

Users subscribe to a monthly plan and receive access to the service. Calls to the virtual number can be forwarded to the user’s phone and managed inside the application.
